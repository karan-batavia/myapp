Critical Requirements to Keep in Mind
A4 Paper Size Only: Ensure your document is NOT set to "US Letter." This is the most common reason for rejection.

Exact Margins: You must maintain white space for RVO stamps:

Left: 2.5 cm (extra space for binding/scanning).

Top, Right, Bottom: 2.0 cm.

Font and Readability: Use a standard, non-proportional font like Courier or Arial. Use 1.5 line spacing to ensure the RVO examiner can clearly read and annotate the text.

No "Rich Content": Do not use colors, hyperlinks, or bold text within the body of the patent description or claims. Keep it plain black and white.

Page Numbering: Numbers must be at the Top Center.

Line Numbering: Must appear in the left margin, typically every 5 lines (5, 10, 15...).

2. Step-by-Step Conversion Process
Step 1: Prepare the Text in a Word Processor
Do not print directly from a Text Editor (like Notepad). Open your .txt file in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or LibreOffice.

Layout: Go to Page Setup and set paper size to A4.

Margins: Set Top: 2cm, Bottom: 2cm, Right: 2cm, and Left: 2.5cm.

Font: Select all text and set to Courier New or Arial, size 12pt. Set line spacing to 1.5.

Step 2: Fix the Page & Line Numbering
Page Numbers: Insert page numbers in the Header (Top Center).

Check: Ensure the numbering is continuous across the whole application (Description ends on pg 3, Claims start on pg 4).

Line Numbers: * In Word: Go to Layout > Line Numbers > Restart Each Page.

Tip: If the numbers appear too far to the left, adjust the "Distance from text" setting so they remain visible within the 2.5cm margin.

Step 3: Handle the Drawings (The Most Important Step)
Do not keep the ASCII art from your .txt file. 1. Open a drawing tool (PowerPoint, Visio, or a free tool like Draw.io). 2. Redraw your diagrams using solid black lines. 3. Label parts with numbers (e.g., "Systeem 100", "Module 102"). 4. Export these as high-resolution images and paste them into a separate document titled "TEKENINGEN".

Step 4: Export to PDF
Go to File > Export > Create PDF/XPS or Save As > PDF.

Optimization: Choose "Standard (publishing online and printing)."

Settings: Ensure "Bitmap text when fonts may not be embedded" is unchecked. RVO needs the text to be searchable (OCR compatible).

Step 5: Final Validation
Open your finished PDF and check:

[ ] Is it A4?

[ ] Are the drawings clear line art (not text characters)?

[ ] Are the page numbers at the top center?

[ ] Is the "Short Title" at the top of the first page identical to the one in your RVO application?
