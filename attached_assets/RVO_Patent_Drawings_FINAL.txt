      BSN CHECKSUM VALIDATIE (Officieel Nederlands Algoritme):
      
105   checksum = (digit_0 × 9) + (digit_1 × 8) + (digit_2 × 7) + 
                 (digit_3 × 6) + (digit_4 × 5) + (digit_5 × 4) + 
                 (digit_6 × 3) + (digit_7 × 2) - (digit_8 × 1)
      
      BSN is geldig als: checksum mod 11 == 0
110   
      Voorbeeld: BSN 111222333
         = (1×9) + (1×8) + (1×7) + (2×6) + (2×5) + (2×4) + (3×3) + (3×2) - (3×1)
         = 9 + 8 + 7 + 12 + 10 + 8 + 9 + 6 - 3
         = 66 mod 11 = 0 ✓ GELDIG
