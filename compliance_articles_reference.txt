ADDITIONAL NETHERLANDS-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- BSN (Burgerservicenummer) Processing Rules
  * Only permitted with explicit legal basis
  * Government services, healthcare, education, financial institutions
  * Strict security measures required
  * Criminal liability for unauthorized use

- AP Guidelines 2024-2025
  * AI decision-making transparency
  * Children's data enhanced protection
  * Biometric processing restrictions
  * Workplace monitoring limitations
  * Cookie consent requirements (strict opt-in)

- Telecommunicatiewet Article 11.7a
  * Cookie consent requirements
  * Pre-ticked boxes prohibited
  * Granular consent required
  * Easy withdrawal mechanism

- 72-Hour Breach Notification
  * Mandatory notification to AP within 72 hours
  * Contact: datalek@autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl
  * Data subject notification without undue delay
  * Breach register maintenance required
