## ENGLISH ABSTRACT

### Title
Unified AI-Driven Semiconductor Validation, Digital Twin, Safety, Yield, and OTA Risk Prediction Pipeline with Chip-Agnostic Plugin Architecture

### Abstract (150 words)

A unified semiconductor validation and lifecycle prediction system comprising: (a) a chip-agnostic plugin architecture where semiconductor device characteristics are defined in structured metadata formats and interpreted by a Large Language Model to dynamically generate validation workflows without code changes; (b) a unified digital twin engine that merges simulation data, EDA signoff results, manufacturing test data, and real-time field telemetry into a continuously evolving representation of chip behavior; (c) a multi-domain analysis engine that correlates functional safety metrics, manufacturing yield data, and AI accelerator performance characteristics; (d) a predictive OTA firmware update risk evaluation module that calculates quantified risk scores based on device health, aging factors, and degradation patterns; and (e) an integrated CI/CD pipeline that orchestrates validation across pre-silicon, silicon bring-up, manufacturing, and field deployment phases. The system enables rapid adaptation to new semiconductor device types and predictive lifecycle management.
