#### DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION

The invention relates to a unified system for semiconductor validation and lifecycle prediction, comprising:

- **Chip-Agnostic Plugin Architecture:** An AI-interpreted metadata system that dynamically generates validation workflows for any semiconductor device type.

- **Unified Digital Twin:** A continuous digital representation integrating simulation, EDA, manufacturing, and field data.

- **Multi-Domain Analysis Engine:** Integrated analysis of functional safety, yield prediction, and AI accelerator performance.

- **OTA Risk Prediction Module:** Predictive evaluation of firmware update risks based on device health and degradation.

- **CI/CD Pipeline:** Automated orchestration of validation across all product lifecycle phases.
