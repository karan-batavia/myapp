PATENT ABSTRACT

A unified system for AI-driven semiconductor validation integrating digital twin technology,
multi-domain analysis, predictive OTA risk assessment, and automated CI/CD pipeline
orchestration. The invention provides chip-agnostic plugin architecture with LLM interpretation
for automatic workflow generation, unified semiconductor digital twins merging EDA simulation,
manufacturing, and field telemetry, cross-domain correlation analysis linking safety, yield,
and performance metrics, quantified OTA firmware update risk scoring based on device health
signals, and end-to-end CI/CD pipeline integration from pre-silicon through field deployment.
The system enables comprehensive lifecycle validation with automated reporting and compliance
certification.

OTA RISK PREDICTION FORMULA

Risk Score Calculation:
R = w_H * H + w_A * A + w_T * T + w_S * S + w_P * P + w_E * E

Where:
  H = Aggregated health score [0.0-1.0]
  A = Aging factor from operational cycles
  T = Thermal stress score from temperature history
  S = Safety margin from current FMEDA state
  P = Performance degradation index
  E = Error history score
  w_x = ML-derived weights learned from historical outcomes

Decision Thresholds:
  R < 0.3:        UPDATE APPROVED
  0.3 <= R < 0.6: UPDATE WITH WARNING
  0.6 <= R < 0.8: UPDATE DEFERRED TO MAINTENANCE
  R >= 0.8:       UPDATE BLOCKED - IMMEDIATE INSPECTION

PATENT VALUE PROPOSITION

The invention addresses the critical need for unified semiconductor validation as chip
complexity increases and AI accelerators proliferate. By integrating digital twin technology
with AI-powered analysis, the system provides unprecedented visibility across the entire
chip lifecycle, enabling predictive maintenance, optimized yield, and safe OTA updates.
The chip-agnostic plugin architecture ensures broad applicability while the LLM-based
workflow generation eliminates manual configuration overhead.
