    PRIOR ART:

    - Synopsys AgentEngineer - Agentic AI for chip design
    - ChipNeMo (NVIDIA) - Domain-adapted LLMs for chip design
    - AssertLLM - Multi-LLM for SVA assertion generation

    PRIOR ART:

    - Synopsys Electronics Digital Twin - Virtual prototyping
    - Tokyo Electron Equipment Twins - Equipment-level only
    - Ansys Semiconductor DT - Physics-based simulation
    - NIST CHIPS Initiative - $285M R&D funding (research, not product)
    
    DIFFERENTIATION: Existing twins are PHASE-SPECIFIC (design OR manufacturing
    OR field). Your claim is for UNIFIED twin merging all three layers with
    bidirectional synchronization and predictive state generation.
    
    VERDICT: NOVEL - Unified lifecycle approach is new

(c) Multi-Domain Analysis Engine (Safety + Yield + Performance)
    PRIOR ART:
    - OneSpin FMEDA - Automated safety validation (safety only)
    - US Patent 10,853,545 - Automatic gate-level FMEDA (safety only)
    - US20190286983A1 - ML yield prediction (yield only)
    - Various CNN bottleneck analysis tools (performance only)
    
    DIFFERENTIATION: Existing tools operate in SILOS. No prior art for
    CROSS-DOMAIN CORRELATION linking safety metrics to yield predictions
    to performance degradation for unified root cause analysis.
    
    VERDICT: NOVEL - Cross-domain integration is unique

(d) OTA Firmware Update Risk Prediction
    PRIOR ART:
    - Memfault - Fleet health monitoring (no quantified risk scoring)
    - Mender - Phased deployments (no device health integration)
    - Various automotive OTA patents - Focus on SECURITY, not HEALTH
    - WO2023108566A1 - OTA management (no risk scoring algorithm)
    
    DIFFERENTIATION: NO PRIOR ART for quantified risk score (0.0-1.0)
    based on 6 weighted device health signals (H, A, T, S, P, E) with
    ML-learned weights from historical update outcomes.
    
    VERDICT: HIGHLY NOVEL - Strongest unique element

(e) Integrated CI/CD Pipeline
    PRIOR ART:
    - Quest Global - Validation services across phases (services, not system)
    - GitHub/GitLab CI/CD - Software-focused
    - EDA vendor flows - Phase-specific
    
    DIFFERENTIATION: No prior art for unified orchestration from pre-silicon
    verification through field deployment with automated quality gates having
    quantified criteria across all semiconductor lifecycle phases.
    
    VERDICT: NOVEL in semiconductor lifecycle context
