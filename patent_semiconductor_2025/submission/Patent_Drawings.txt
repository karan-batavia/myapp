FIGURE 6: MATHEMATICAL FORMULAS


OTA Risk Score Algorithm:

Formula 1 - Risk Score Calculation:
R = w_H * H + w_A * A + w_T * T + w_S * S + w_P * P + w_E * E

Where weights sum to 1.0:
sum(w_H, w_A, w_T, w_S, w_P, w_E) = 1.0

Formula 2 - ML Weight Learning:
w_optimal = argmin_w [ L(outcomes, predictions(w)) ]
Subject to: sum(w) = 1.0, w_i >= 0



Safety Metrics (ISO 26262):

Formula 3 - Single Point Fault Metric:
SPFM = 1 - (lambda_SPF / lambda_total)

Formula 4 - Latent Fault Metric:
LFM = 1 - (lambda_LF / lambda_total)

Formula 5 - PMHF (Probabilistic Metric for Hardware Failures):
PMHF = sum(lambda_i * (1 - DC_i) * exposure_time_i)



Yield Prediction Models:

Formula 6 - Poisson Yield Model:
Y = exp(-D * A)
Where D = defect density, A = die area

Formula 7 - Negative Binomial Model:
Y = (1 + D*A/alpha)^(-alpha)
Where alpha = clustering parameter

Formula 8 - GMM Clustering:
P(x) = sum_k [ pi_k * N(x | mu_k, Sigma_k) ]



Digital Twin Synchronization:

Formula 9 - State Evolution:
S(t+1) = f(S(t), telemetry(t), simulation(t))

Formula 10 - Bidirectional Update:
delta_twin = alpha * (observed - predicted)
twin_state += delta_twin
