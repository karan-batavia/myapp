                                      FIGUUR 6


     +-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
     |                     MATHEMATISCHE FORMULES                            |
     +-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
     | Nr  | Naam                        | Formule                           |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 1   | Risicoscore Berekening      | R = w_H*H + w_A*A + w_T*T +       |
     |     |                             |     w_S*S + w_P*P + w_E*E         |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 2   | Gewichtsvoorwaarde          | som(w_H,w_A,w_T,w_S,w_P,w_E) = 1.0|
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 3   | ML Gewichtsleren            | w_opt = argmin_w[L(uit,voorsp(w))]|
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 4   | Single Point Fault Metric   | SPFM = 1 - (lambda_SPF/lambda_tot)|
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 5   | Latent Fault Metric         | LFM = 1 - (lambda_LF/lambda_tot)  |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 6   | PMHF                        | PMHF = som(lambda_i*(1-DC_i)*t_i) |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 7   | Poisson Opbrengstmodel      | Y = exp(-D * A)                   |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 8   | Negatief Binomiaal Model    | Y = (1 + D*A/alpha)^(-alpha)      |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 9   | GMM Clustering              | P(x) = som_k[pi_k*N(x|mu_k,Sig_k)]|
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 10  | Toestandsevolutie           | S(t+1) = f(S(t),telemetrie(t),    |
     |     |                             |          simulatie(t))            |
     +-----+-----------------------------+-----------------------------------+
     | 11  | Bidirectionele Update       | delta = alpha*(waargenomen-voorsp)|
     |     |                             | tweeling_toestand += delta        |
